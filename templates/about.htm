What Goes, is a website and forthcoming app, that demonstrates how one piece of clothing works three ways - on tastemakers in music, art and fashion.

What Goes creates looks on influencers from photographers to painters, writers to designers, musicians to actors. The experience can be described as a journey to a place of choice, collaborating to style the ultimate look, with an interview of the trailblazer to follow. A fashion-focused, insightful and fun project, What Goes is sartorial meets visionary, an “Inside the Doers Studio” for those innovative individuals.
